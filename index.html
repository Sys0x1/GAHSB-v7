Welcome to Golden Angels Holdings Sdn. Bhd.

“Innovation, integrity, and efficiency define us."

Golden Angels Holdings Sdn. Bhd. is a dynamic industry leader specializing in demolition, civil works, and steel fabrication. With a steadfast commitment to quality, safety, and innovation, we deliver top tier solutions tailored to meet the demands of modern infrastructure projects. Our expertise spans large-scale demolitions, precision civil engineering, and high-quality steel fabrication, making us a trusted partner for government and private sector projects.

Founded on principles of excellence and efficiency, Golden Angels Holdings Sdn. Bhd. has rapidly grown into a trusted name in Malaysia’s construction and demolition sector. Our company is built on a foundation of technical expertise, strategic execution, and a client-centric approach. Over the years, we have completed numerous projects that showcase our capability to handle complex challenges while adhering to the highest industry standards.
